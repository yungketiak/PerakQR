How It Works

From Street Corner to
Digital Story

PerakQR turns the lived history of every heritage business into a digital experience — one scan at a time.

01

SME Registers

Local business owners — particularly elderly operators — register their shop, stall, or craft on PerakQR with their story, history, and offerings.

02

Story is Crafted

Our content team conducts an on-ground interview to document the authentic narrative: founding year, family legacy, cultural significance, and what makes them irreplaceable.

03

QR Goes Live

A unique QR code is generated and placed at the business. Scan it and instantly access the full digital profile — photos, story, menu, and directions.

04

Visitors Discover

Tourists and locals browse PerakQR to plan their visits, find hidden gems, and experience Perak's heritage through the eyes of the people who built it.

Digital Storytelling

Not Just a Directory.
A Living Archive.

Every PerakQR listing is a story — the kind that gets lost when a shop closes or an owner passes on. We document what Google Maps can't: the why behind the what.

On-ground Story Collection

Our field team conducts real interviews with business owners — capturing voice, history, and sentiment that no form can replicate.

Living History That Updates

Owners update their own pages — new dishes, seasonal offerings, apprentice stories — keeping the archive alive and current.

Intergenerational Handover

When the next generation takes over, the story continues — not starts fresh. Built-in succession storytelling for family businesses.
